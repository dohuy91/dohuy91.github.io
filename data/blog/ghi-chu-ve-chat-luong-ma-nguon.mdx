---
title: Ghi Chu Ve Chat Luong Ma Nguon
date: '2026-03-01'
language: 'vi'
tags: ['chat-luong', 'review', 'ky-thuat']
summary: Mot vai nguyen tac thuc dung de giu code de doc, de review, va de thay doi trong du an lon.
---

Chat luong ma nguon khong den tu viec theo duoi moi mau kien truc moi. No den tu kha nang giai thich ro rang tai sao mot doan code ton tai va no duoc thay doi nhu the nao.

Trong cac du an lon, toi uu tien ba dieu:

1. Moi file nen co mot muc dich ro rang.
2. Du lieu di qua cac tang nen de lan theo.
3. Reviewer co the xac dinh rui ro ma khong can doc ca module.

Neu mot thay doi can sua o qua nhieu noi, do thuong la dau hieu cho thay ranh gioi trach nhiem chua tot. Khi do, cai can toi uu khong phai la them comment, ma la tach logic cho de hieu hon.
